Jimena Arruti is currently pursuing her MSc in Sound and Music Computing at Queen Mary University of London and working as a Research Assistant at C4DM, QMUL. She holds an Electrical Engineering BSc from Universidad de la República (Uruguay), focused on Signal Processing and Machine learning. She has previously worked in industry as an R&D Engineer and in education as a Lecturer and teaching assistant. She is also a composer and musician, and she focus her research on creative tools.

During her time at the Dynamical Intelligence Systems Lab, she has collaborated with the EPSRC AI through music project, creating teaching materials for high school students to learn Artificial Intelligence concepts through musical applications. She is also developing the lab's website.
